EMF & OCL Domains: Model Integrity (cont.)
§ The ModelValidationService
singleton coordinates the invocation of
validation.  It defines a single factory
method for creation of IValidator for
the batch and live evaluation modes.
§ Validators validate one or more objects
at a time; the kind of object accepted
as input depends on the evaluation
mode.  They can be configured to
report constraint passes as well as
failures, for verbose results.
§ Results are reported as
IValidationStatus.
§ Validators can be reused by a client for
any number of validation operations.
§ The ILiveValidator validates EMF
Notifications.
§ The IBatchValidator validates
EObjects and, due to its support for
model traversal, supports progress
monitors.
§ Registered traversal strategies can
be overridden by the client.
