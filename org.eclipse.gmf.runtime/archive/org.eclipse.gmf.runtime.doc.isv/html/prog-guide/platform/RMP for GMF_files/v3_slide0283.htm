Common Domain: Providers
§ Each service has one or more service providers.
§ Providers are register with the service by implementing the provider
extension point.
§ The provider extension point specifies the provider and it’s priority.
§ Each provider registers a priority.
§ Prioritization provides a means of selecting one or more providers
from a list of candidate providers based on their importance.
4 HIGHEST: signifies a highest ordered provider.
4 LOWEST: signifies a lowest ordered provider.
